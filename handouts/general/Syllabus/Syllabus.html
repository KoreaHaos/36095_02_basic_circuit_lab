1. Course Description


This course mandates attendees to utilize discrete semiconductor devices such as diodes, BJTs, and MOSFETs, 
to design amplifiers and Op-amps, and to conduct various labs for testing. 
Also, computer simulations using PSPICE should be performed in each lab. 
to understand the allocated micro-electronic circuits and furthermore to be capable of conducting projects.
